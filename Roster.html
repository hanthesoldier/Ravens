Roster

The Baltimore Ravens roster refers to the group of players who are officially part of the team in the National Football League, including both starters and backups across offense, defense, and special teams. On offense, the roster is led by quarterback Lamar Jackson, along with running backs, wide receivers, tight ends, and offensive linemen who work together to score points. On defense, the roster includes positions like defensive linemen, linebackers, and defensive backs whose job is to stop the opposing team from scoring. Special teams players handle kicking, punting, and returns. An NFL roster typically has a limited number of active players for each game, along with additional players on the practice squad who can be called up if needed. Overall, the Ravens roster is carefully built to balance talent, depth, and teamwork so the team can compete at a high level throughout the season.
