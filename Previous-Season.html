Previous Season Records

The Baltimore Ravens had a challenging 2025 season in the National Football League, finishing with an 8–9 record and placing second in the AFC North, which caused them to miss the playoffs for the first time since 2021. The season started poorly, as the team struggled early and fell behind in the standings, making it difficult to recover despite some solid offensive production, including over 400 total points scored . While players like Lamar Jackson continued to lead the offense, inconsistency and tough losses—especially at home, where they had a losing record—hurt their chances of success . One of the biggest storylines was the end of an era, as longtime head coach John Harbaugh was fired after the season, marking a major change for the franchise . Overall, the Ravens’ previous season was seen as disappointing compared to their strong 2024 performance, but it set the stage for rebuilding and adjustments going forward.
