M&T Bank Stadium

M&T Bank Stadium is the home of the Baltimore Ravens and one of the most exciting stadiums in the NFL. Located in Baltimore, Maryland, it opened in 1998 and can hold over 70,000 fans. The stadium is known for its loud atmosphere, especially during big games, where Ravens fans create a powerful home-field advantage. It features modern seating, large video screens, and great views from almost every seat. With its electric energy and passionate crowd, M&T Bank Stadium is a key part of the Ravens’ identity and game-day experience.

Stadium Facts

  • Opened in 1998 – Home of the Baltimore Ravens since day one.
  • Holds about 70,745 fans.
  • Cost around $220 million to build.
  • Located in Baltimore, Maryland.
  • Nicknamed “The Bank”.
  • Built in less than 2 years.
  • Features large video screens and modern technology.
  • Includes luxury suites and premium seating.
  • Environmentally friendly with green certification.
  • Continues to be upgraded and renovated.
